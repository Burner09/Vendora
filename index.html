Executive Summary

Vendora is a local e-commerce hub designed to connect consumers with businesses and individuals throughout the country. Our mission is to empower all sellers ranging from small local shops to individuals offering unique products by providing a platform that enhances visibility and simplifies the shopping experience.

Target Market

Sellers:

  • Local Businesses: Restaurants, retail shops, and service providers seeking to expand their reach.
  • Individuals: Local artisans, crafters, and anyone looking to sell unique products.
  • Challenges: Many sellers need assistance in managing their online presence effectively.

Consumers:

  • Individuals and Families: Local shoppers seeking convenience and variety.
  • Community Supporters: Consumers motivated to support local sellers and businesses.
  • Convenience Seekers: Looking for an easy way to access a wide range of local offerings.

Feasibility Analysis

Product/Service Feasibility

Vendora provides an integrated marketplace where sellers can create profiles, list products, and interact with customers. This approach facilitates sales and offers insights to help sellers make informed decisions.

Industry/Target Market Feasibility

The local e-commerce landscape is evolving, with increasing demand for accessible shopping options. Vendora is well-positioned to meet the diverse needs of consumers while supporting a wide range of sellers.

Business Model

Vendora operates on a New-Market Disruption model, serving as a hub for all sellers, both businesses and individuals. Our focus is on empowering local enterprises and individuals to thrive in a digital marketplace while enhancing community engagement.

Competitive Analysis

Vendora connects consumers to small businesses for food, products or services providing experiences that feel personalized. In contrast many of Vendora's competitors typically offer only one service like food delivery or e-commerce unlike Vendora's goal of a one stop hub.
